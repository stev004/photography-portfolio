# Mockups — The Print Room & The Hang

Self-contained HTML pages (all images embedded as data URIs — just open in a browser).

## print-room.html — Mockup Series 01–03
Sixteen print treatments built from the strongest frames in the archive:
magazine cover (Undergrowth), exhibition poster (Eight Eyes), broadsheet
(The Field Press), film zine (Grain No. 3), botanical plates (Hortus),
art print (Cavallino), rally poster (Night Stage), LP sleeve (Neroport),
book jacket (The Quiet Field), travel spread (Roads South), postcards,
gallery print (Four Boats), wall calendar (MMXXVII), coffee label
(Eight Eyes Espresso), film one-sheet (South, After Sunset), and a gig
poster (The Salticids).

## the-hang.html — Exhibition mocks
- **Edition 01 — Field Work**: flagship two-room show (16 works), rendered
  wall-by-wall to scale, with floor plan, hang list, guide pricing and a
  practical run-through (printing, framing, hanging, lighting, labels, budget).
- **Edition 02 — Eight Eyes**: the all-spider show (9 works, one dark room).
- **The Season**: census of the full archive grouped into six possible shows
  (Field Work, Eight Eyes, Roads South, Vivarium, Hortus, Paddock) with
  readiness ratings.
