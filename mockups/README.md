## the-hang.html — Exhibition mocks, Editions 01–05

Wall-by-wall hang renders to scale (real print sizes), each with curator
notes:

- **Edition 01 — Field Work**: flagship two-room show (16 works) with floor
  plan, hang list, guide pricing and a practical run-through (printing,
  framing, hanging heights, lighting, labels, budget).
- **Edition 02 — Eight Eyes**: the all-spider show (9 works, one dark room).
- **Edition 03 — Vivarium**: reptile-house portraits (6 works, dark room).
- **Edition 04 — Roads South**: the 35mm diary hung as one day of light
  (11 works, gallery white).
- **Edition 05 — Paddock**: motor-event pop-up on scaffold & ply (6 works).
- **The Season**: census of the portfolio grouped into six possible shows
  with readiness ratings. Hortus remains unmocked pending a spring of
  flower shooting.

## second-edit.html — Desktop film archive census (reference)

Grading of the 371 unassessed film frames on the desktop (rolls 0321–0327 +
Order 340382). Kept as a working record of what's in the archive and where
it lives — Steven's verdict is that these frames are mostly not
portfolio-grade (blown highlights / underexposed), so treat the "A-list"
here as archive notes, not a pull list. The digital Lightroom catalogue
(laptop) is the next census.
